They Stole Nobloxia
From Us.

This is the story of how a fake "company" called Azure Ventures conned me and my team out of a group we built from the ground up.

What was Nobloxia

A group we built together

Nobloxia was a Roblox group created and grown by me and my team. We put real time, effort, and trust into building it — developing games, growing a community, and working toward something we were proud of.

What happened

Azure Ventures moved in

A group calling themselves Azure Ventures approached us under the guise of a professional partnership or acquisition. What followed was a deliberate and calculated act of theft — they took ownership of Nobloxia and cut us out entirely, leaving me and my team with nothing after everything we had put into it.

Azure Ventures is not a legitimate company. They used the appearance of professionalism to gain trust, then exploited it. Do not do business with them.

Why this page exists

People deserve to know

I'm not making this page out of anger — I'm making it because the Roblox development community deserves to know who Azure Ventures is and what they did. If you've been approached by them, or by DevMammal or DevFleas, be careful. What happened to us can happen to anyone who lets their guard down.
